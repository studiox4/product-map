Not a map company / a planning tool

Reset. Product Map isn't about geography — it's where teams design projects, stay aligned, document decisions, see what's next, and organize their own roadmaps and Gantt charts. So these marks drop the location-pin and speak the real language: sequencing, priority, alignment, progress, the system of work. Concept 01 brings back your original Gantt-of-PM idea — refined — because for this product it's the most honest.

The job to be done

Make the messy plan legible: what we're building, in what order, why it matters, and whether we agree. The logo should feel like clarity, not coordinates.

Visual vocabulary

Bars, lanes, nodes, axes, checkpoints, a north star. Structure and sequence — the shapes of planning — not pins and terrain.

How to choose

Which single idea do you want to own? Roadmap (sequence), North Star (alignment), or Value Map (priority) are the three strongest bets.
