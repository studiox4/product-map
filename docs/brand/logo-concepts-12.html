Can the bars spell PM?

The original idea: P and M built from gantt bars. The catch — a real gantt is horizontal-only, but P and M need uprights, so pure-horizontal letters go pixel-y (that's why v1 felt cheap). Below, three honest attempts, each tested at 32 and 20px — because a monogram that only works big is no good as a favicon. If none beat it, the no-letter Stagger / Now still wins; both are shown at the bottom for the call.
