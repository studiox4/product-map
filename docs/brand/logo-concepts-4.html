Eight real directions / not recolors

The brief is two ideas fighting for the logo: Product (a roadmap, a system, phases) and Map (a place, a route, coordinates). Each concept below picks a different visual strategy to resolve that — pin, route, contour, isometric stack, emblem, UI-glyph, folded paper, logotype. Distinct shapes, distinct construction, distinct color logic. Pick the strategy first; color and type are tunable after.

What changed

v3 was one bar-generator recolored eight times. Same shape ≠ eight ideas. Every mark here is built from scratch with its own geometry.

How to read it

Judge the icon at small size first (the favicon strip proves it). A logo that survives 16px wins. Color is the last decision, not the first.

The throughline

Location + system. Most marks fuse a map signal (pin / route / coordinate) with a product signal (phases / nodes / structure).
