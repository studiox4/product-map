Forward / the Product Map mark

The other finalist, taken to the same finish as Pivot — so the choice is between two complete systems, not a sketch and a system. Forward is a roadmap of timeline bars tuned so the whole silhouette becomes a ► — "what's next." The signature device is the stepped bar: a stack of plan rows whose centre row (the live next item) carries the lit accent. That same shadow/lit logic runs through the wordmark and system.

01 — The mark · primary + mono
__PRIMARY__

__MONO__

__REV__

Refinement from v8. The face-off used 5 equal bars. Canonical Forward keeps 5 (the cleanest arrow + clearest "roadmap" read), pill caps at exactly half-height, and a lit centre bar so the apex doubles as the live next item. 4 bars get blunt; 6 muddy the point.
