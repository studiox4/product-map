Stagger / the Product Map mark

The one that won. Stagger is the literal gantt — bars beginning at different times, in different lengths — the truest picture of a roadmap, and the cleanest descendant of the original P-and-M-in-bars idea. This sheet locks its geometry, the wordmark and lockups, colour, motion, the rules, and ships the files. The device is two streams: a shadow tone and a lit tone alternating down the timeline.

01 — The mark · primary + mono

Refinement. Locked at four bars — enough to read as a timeline, few enough to stay crisp at favicon size. Starts and lengths are tuned (not random) so the silhouette balances optically around the centre. 3 bars feels thin; 5 starts to clutter.

04 — Wordmark & lockups
